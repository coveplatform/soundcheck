## Recommendations

### 1. Create `/alternatives/submithub` — highest-opportunity gap

"SubmitHub alternatives" surfaces a cluster of results (PlaylistPilot, Groover, NotNoise, Magnetic Mag) with no MixReflect in sight. MixReflect is a natural answer to this query: artists using SubmitHub want real human listener feedback, which is the core product. A `/alternatives/submithub` page should:
- Lead with the key differentiator (AI score + 5 real listeners vs. curator-only pitching)
- Include a clear comparison table (SubmitHub fee structure vs. MixReflect pricing)
- Target the title "SubmitHub Alternative: Get Listener Feedback Before You Pitch"
- Internally link to the homepage, `/alternatives/groover` (if published), and the roundup blog post

This page is also directly queried by artists who are already frustrated with SubmitHub — the highest-intent, lowest-friction audience for MixReflect.

### 2. Push the "ai music feedback" blog post to rank — it exists but isn't visible

The blog post "Best AI Tools for Music Feedback in 2026" was published 2026-06-24, targeting the keyword `ai music feedback`. It is not yet appearing in search results. Competitors ranking include Slapback.io, Mix Check Studio, and Rate My Song AI — all of which the post should be covering. Actions to improve ranking:
- Verify the post is indexed (GSC → Coverage → Inspect URL)
- Strengthen the opening paragraph as a standalone quotable answer for AEO/AI Overviews: "The best AI music feedback tools in 2026 are…"
- Add internal links to this post from the homepage FAQ and from genre pages (currently a sitemap-orphan risk)
- Per SEO-CHECKLIST.md item E: confirm `GPTBot`, `PerplexityBot`, and `ClaudeBot` are not blocked in `robots.txt`

### 3. Target "rate my track" with a dedicated landing page or stronger homepage signal

MixReflect literally rates tracks (AI score 0–100) but is invisible for "rate my track" and related queries. Competitors showing here (RateMySong.ai, PlusMetrica) are pure AI-only raters with no human element — MixReflect's hybrid positioning is a differentiator. Options:
- Add a short `/rate-my-track` landing page (or redirect to homepage) with title "Rate My Track — Get an AI Score + 5 Real Listeners | MixReflect"
- At minimum, add "rate my track" / "rate my song" as secondary keywords in the homepage `<title>` or H1 subhead, and in the FAQ block
- Blog post "Is My Song Good? An Honest Way to Tell Before You Release" (currently ⏳ in queue) directly targets this intent — prioritise it next in the content queue
