  the calendar's DONE log + alternatives + the AI-tools post), 32 genre pages,
  9 alternatives pages.

- Verify logged-out `/submit-score` → `/api/score/start` still works post-auth-overhaul. The created=0 cliff is the single most important number in this audit.

- Per-page OG images for genre/alternatives.
- Reddit/community presence (help-first) for referring domains.
- Monthly GEO/AI-citation check (already scheduled).

## 5. What to expect (trajectory)

- **Indexing/ranking:** the content layer is young (most pages < 4 weeks old).
  Expect genre + "is my song good / ready" intent pages to start surfacing in
  positions 8-20 over the next 4-8 weeks, then climb as internal linking + freshness compound. Hub pages would accelerate this.
- **Organic → revenue:** at the current 27.5% scored→paid, even modest organic
  submit volume monetizes well. The lever is **submissions**, not conversion.
  Fix the funnel cliff and organic content has somewhere to convert.
- **Honest ceiling:** without real GSC data we're flying on structure +
  inference. Wire up Search Console (see §6) to replace this with measured
  positions and find the position 8-15 "one title tweak = page-one" wins.
